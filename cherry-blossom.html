Why I Chose Cherry Blossoms

Cherry blossoms inspired the light pink and hot pink colors used throughout this website.

The flower creates a calm, fresh, and spring-like mood that fits the floral theme.

🌸

Design Connection

  • Light pink represents blossom petals
  • Hot pink creates contrast and energy
  • Light green connects to gardens and leaves
  • Rounded shapes make the design feel organic
